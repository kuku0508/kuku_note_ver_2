# 推導過程
$$
\begin{align}
B&=2\sqrt{Var(\bar{y})}=2\sqrt{\frac{\sigma^2}{n}\cdot\frac{N-n}{N-1}}\\\\
&\text{假設}D=\frac{B^2}{4}\\

&\Rightarrow\frac{\sigma^2}{n}\cdot\frac{N-n}{N-1}=D\\\\


\end{align}
$$
