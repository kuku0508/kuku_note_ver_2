# 問題：
For the $2^2$ factorial with three observations per cell given below(hypotheical data), do an analysis
$$
\begin{array}{l|rr}
&A_1&A_2\\
\hline
B_1&0&4\\
&2&6\\
&1&2\\
B_2&-1&-1\\
&-3&-3\\
&1&-7
\end{array}
$$
# 回答：
畫正交表
沒有那個因子的就負號、有的就正號
$$
\begin{array}{l|lll|l}
&A&B&AB&Y_i\\
\hline
(1)&-&-&+&(A_1\bigcap B_1)=0+2+1=3\\
A&+&-&-&(A_2\bigcap B_1)=4+6+2=12\\
B&-&+&-&(A_1\bigcap B_2)=-1-3+1=-3\\
AB&+&+&+&(A_2\bigcap B_2)=-1-3-7=-11
\end{array}
$$
有上面的正負號，我們就去計算$C_i$
$$
C_A=-3+12+3-11=1
C_B=-3-12-3-11=-29
C_{AB}=3-12+3-11=-17
SS_A=\frac{}{}
$$
