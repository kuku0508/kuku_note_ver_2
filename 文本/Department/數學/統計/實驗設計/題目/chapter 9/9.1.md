# 問題：
For the $2^2$ factorial with three observations per cell given below(hypotheical data), do an analysis
$$
\begin{array}{l|rr}
&A_1&A_2\\
\hline
B_1&0&4\\
&2&6\\
&1&2\\
B_2&-1&-1\\
&-3&-3\\
&1&-7
\end{array}
$$
# 回答：
畫正交表
沒有那個因子的就負號、有的就正號
$$
\begin{array}{l|lll|l}
&A&B&AB&Y_i\\
\hline
(1)&-&-&+\\
A&+&-&-\\
B&-&+&-\\
AB&+&+&+
\end{array}
$$
