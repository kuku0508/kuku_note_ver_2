$$
X_1,X_2\sim Poisson(\lambda),\lambda>0
$$
試問
$$
A(X_1,X_2)=1\lbrace X_1<X_2\rbrace
$$
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{e^{-\lambda}\lambda^{X_1}}{X_1!}<\frac{e^{-\lambda}\lambda^{X_2}}{X_2!}\\\\
1>\frac{\frac{\lambda^{X_1}}{X_1!}}{\frac{\lambda^{X_2}}{X_2!}}\\\\
1>\frac{\lambda^{X_1-X_2}\cdot X_2!}{X_1!}
\end{align}
$$
