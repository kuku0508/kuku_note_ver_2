# 問題：
The joint pdf of the r.v's $X$ and $Y$ is given by：
$$
f_{X,Y}(x,y)=cx,\quad x>0,\quad y>0,\quad 1\leq x+y<2\quad (c>0)
$$
Determine the constant c.
